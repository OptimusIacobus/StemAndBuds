Our Story...

In the summer of 2017, Victoria and Ashna met each other at a speech and debate summer camp. After talking about high school and their own experiences, the two realized they loved teaching and fields within STEM. A couple phone calls, emails, and texts later...STEM & Buds was created. And the rest is history.

VOLUNTEERS

STEM & Buds is entirely run by dedicated high school volunteers that are passionate about STEM and enthusiastic to help younger students. Serving as chapter heads and mentors, our volunteers have built a lasting network of devoted teens working to improve STEM education in our city.
